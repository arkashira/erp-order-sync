# Partner Targets
## Overview
As a key component of the ERP-order-sync product, integrating with relevant SaaS and APIs will enhance the tool's functionality and user experience. This document outlines the partner targets, integration roadmap, and specific APIs to integrate.

## Integration Roadmap
### Phase 1: Initial Integration (Weeks 1-4)
- Integrate with 3-4 key SaaS/APIs to demonstrate the product's capabilities and gather feedback from early adopters.

### Phase 2: Expansion and Optimization (Weeks 5-12)
- Integrate with an additional 4-5 SaaS/APIs to further enhance the product's functionality and user experience.

### Phase 3: Maintenance and Support (After Week 12)
- Continuously monitor and update existing integrations to ensure seamless performance and compatibility.

## Partner Targets
### SaaS/APIs to Integrate
